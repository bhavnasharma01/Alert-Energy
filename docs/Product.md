# Product

## Business Context

Alert Energy is an energy consulting firm founded in 2014, serving utilities, engineering firms, and building owners across North America. The firm specializes in building energy services including M&V, regulated benchmarking, energy auditing, and commissioning.

## Website Purpose

The website serves as the primary lead generation tool for Alert Energy's "compliance wedge" marketing strategy: lead with regulated benchmarking as a specific, urgent service, then expand clients into the full lifecycle offering.

## Target Audience

**Primary:** Building owners who need help with regulated energy benchmarking in five cities with upcoming compliance deadlines:

| City | Deadline | Regulation |
|------|----------|------------|
| Washington, D.C. | May 1, 2026 | BEPS |
| Chicago, IL | June 1, 2026 | Energy Benchmarking Ordinance (Ch. 18-4) |
| Denver, CO | June 1, 2026 | Energize Denver |
| Vancouver, BC | June 1, 2026 | Building Benchmark BC |
| Toronto, ON | July 1, 2026 | EWRB / EGPS |

**Secondary:** Utilities and engineering firms seeking energy consulting partners.

## Key Positioning

- "Benchmarking is a requirement. Accuracy is an investment."
- "Optimizing Energy Since 2014"
- M&V experts who apply IPMVP standards (not a low-cost data entry service)
- Institutional-grade precision (Westfield, Macy's, Mandarin Oriental) available to all building owners
- City-specific expertise, not one-size-fits-all

## Notable Clients

- Westfield Properties (multi-property portfolio energy compliance)
- Macy's (enterprise-scale energy accounting and benchmarking)
- Mandarin Oriental Hotels (premium hospitality energy management)

## Phased Roadmap

### Phase 1 -- Launch (Current)
- 4-page static site: Home, Benchmarking, About, Contact
- Dynamic compliance deadline countdown
- Contact form (Formspree) + Calendly booking
- Deploy to Netlify at alertenergy.net

### Phase 2 -- Expand (4-6 weeks post-launch)
- City-specific landing pages: `/dc`, `/chicago`, `/denver`, `/vancouver`, `/toronto`
- Blog/insights section using Astro content collections
- Case studies page (pending client approval)
- Real brand assets (logo, finalized color palette)

### Phase 3 -- Grow (As business develops)
- Compliance Risk Scorecard (interactive lead-gen tool)
- Full services page for broader offering
- Client portal for filing status tracking
- Resource library with downloadable guides

## Content Guidelines

- No em dashes -- use double hyphens or rewrite
- Use "we" voice (not "they" or "the firm")
- Reference client names near CTAs for trust signals
- City-specific content should demonstrate local knowledge
- Technical terms (M&V, IPMVP, BEPS, EWRB, EGPS) used but briefly explained
- Connect benchmarking back to broader value: "first step in understanding your building's energy health"

## Lead Capture Strategy

Two complementary paths:
1. **Contact form** -- Structured lead info (name, email, city, building size, compliance status) submitted via Formspree
2. **Calendly** -- Self-service booking for 15-minute compliance review calls
